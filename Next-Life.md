# Philosophy begins:

I have a simple philosophy:

Do your work honestly to serve the best don't care much about failure/success or fear of failure. 

We do work not to endlessly earn or achieve something but to live the life by enjoying it. 

Feel no shame if you know you are not doing anything wrong.

Live respectfully and give others equal importance, even to an ant.

Be an agnostic because world if full of infinite possibilities that you can't see. Not to flatter someone but to live neutral.

Be neutral in most cases.

What about when serving others honestly costs you deeply and nobody notices or reciprocates?

"... to serve" was never ment to literally serve something to someone but to serve yourself -- the sole judge of yourself.

Be an atom before being someone at the top. 

We chase merit & sins the entire life thinking I would be this in next life. We follow imagination both negative & positive. We follow our past traumas and emotions.

But we forget nothing comes without responsibility. Even an atom has to just work. Now think about all your fears and desires and see them through the lens of responsibilities. Will you carry them? At first most will say, Yes! But they will not be the same when given the same responsibility. 

That's why it is better to be what you are. Not what other's imagine you or what you want to be or to not be.

First follow your responsibility i.e your work, your life and only after that think about anything else, because it truly will not make sense after some points.
